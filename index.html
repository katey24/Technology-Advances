  • Welcome!

    OurBackground:
    47 counties for peace and vision 2030 is a community based organisation that was born on 4th May,2010 with an intention to move the 47 counties in line with vision 2030 goal of enhancing clean environment, thus health for all. Our organisation will contribute in achievingthis goal by enhancing cleaniness of one of the most basic facility in every habitat, yet one of the most neglected, especially the communal ones like those in shcools, towns and instutions. The organisation shall endavor to enhance best practices of toilet hygiene in schools in Kenya and shall extend its services to the community in future. It is our wish to continue with this project and further expand our reach.

    What We Offer

    47Countries For Peace and Vision 2030 is a humanitarian movement that responds to the plight of the minority but, all the same significant memebrs of our rural areas. And here at vision 2030 plan you can find details of how these movement has impacted a large number of people in many rural areas and our expectations.

  • Mission & Vision

    Mission

    To enhance healthy and conducive environment in public schools in Knya by promoting dynamics through adopting the best practices in toilet cleaning by use of detergents, economical utilization of available resources such as water to maintain clean toilets and hand washing after toilet use. Utilization and improvisation of locally available materials to constuct decent and modern toilets that meet the needs of the locals, offering clean toiletry/hygiene consultation services and talk shows to schools and locals, and to networking with other environmetal friendly organisations both locally and internationally to reach and sponsor our clients.

    Vision

    To be the Leading community based organisation group in enhancing clean environemnt in schools.
